Nairobi

Nairobi is kenyan's capital city. In addition to its urban core,the city has Nairobi
National park, a large reserve known for breeding endangered black rhinos
and home to giraffes, zebras and lions.

Next to is a well-regarded elephant ophanage operated by the David Sheldrick
wildlife Trust. Nairobi is also often used as a jumping-off for safari trips
elsewhere in kenya
